Is Mercury in Retrograde?

What is Mercury Retrograde?

Mercury Retrograde is a period of time when Mercury, the planet of communication and travel, appears to be moving backwards in its orbit. Mercury Retrograde occurs three to four times a year and can last anywhere from 18 to 24 days. During Mercury Retrograde, it is common to experience communication problems, travel delays, and technical difficulties.
